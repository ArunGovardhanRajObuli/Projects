# SQL Business Analysis of the SuperStoreOrders dataset.

## Goal
Answering key business questions using SQL.

## Database
SQLite database created from cleaned sales data.

## Questions Answered
1. Total monthly sales.
2. Top 10 customers by revenue.
3. Average order value.
4. Repeat customers.
5. Most profitable category.
6. Region with highest profit margin.
7. Products that gave loss.
8. Monthly growth rate.
9. Most profitable shipping method.
10. Customer's lifetime value.

## Insights
- Some products consistently lose money.
- Many of the customers come back to purchase again.
- Office supplies are the most profitable category.
- Standard shipping yields the most profit.
- The most profitable region is Cannada.
